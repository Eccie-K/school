UPEO SCHOOL



ARTS


The Arts are key in supporting children to develop in these areas.
The term ‘the Arts’ includes; music (instrumental and vocal), dance, drama, creative writing,
painting and sculpture, photography, costume design, motion pictures and radio. At Upeo School,
we want to promote an awareness and enthusiasm for the visual arts, music, dance and drama and
increase the opportunities for participation through:

  • The teaching of skills across all arts subjects and through topic work
  • Enabling all children to take part in performances, involving a variety of audiences.
  • The provision of a range of visits (galleries, local museums, theatres etc.) and visitors (musicians, visiting artists etc).
Every child has the opportunity to take part in two large performances (one in key Stage 1 and one in Key Stage 2),
as well as smaller performances through other areas of learning.
All children learn the recorder in key Stage 2. There are also a variety of music lessons available including guitar, woodwind and violin.
We have specialist teachers for music and fine art, giving the children the opportunity to learn skills from experts.
Plus ,each topic has a creative element to it, where children will use skills taught in the context of their current learning.
We also have enrichment clubs, which children can join. These include;
  • Art
  • Dance
  • Choir
  • Street dance
  • Museum and coding.
