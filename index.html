WELCOME TO UPEO SCHOOL


Upeo School is a truly special place to learn together.
At Upeo School, we strive as much as possible to ensure that
our students achieve individual potential while at the same time
developing respect and admiration for others.

We have high expectations of our students' learning and behaviour.
Team work is at the heart of everything we do. We work together
to provide every child with an inclusive, high quality, enjoyable
experience where they feel appreciated and valued.
