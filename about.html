OUR STORY



History

Upeo School was founded in the year 2000 by Ben Chaka as a small preparatory school in the outskirts of Nairobi. It is located in Springfield Estate, Grivillea groove in Brookside,Westlands.


Our Mission

To embrace holistic learning that is geared towards addressing academic,social,mental,psychological,ethical and spiritual wellbeing of leaners.


Our Vision

To empower our learners to acquire,demonstrate,articulate,and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life long learners, to participate and contribute to the global world.
